Welcome to Utility Tool Box, developed by Akshit Negi. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data and permissions within the app.

1. Information We Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information with third parties. However, the app requests certain permissions for its features to function properly:

Permission Purpose
Internet Access Used for displaying ads and updating app content.
Microphone Used only to test microphone functionality within the app. No audio is stored or shared.
Camera Required for scanning QR codes.
Storage Used for saving compressed images, generated QR codes, ZIP files, and related outputs.
Location (Approximate) Used for detecting nearby devices for Wi-Fi Direct sharing.
Phone (Call Permission) Allows calling the emergency contact numbers provided by the user. No calls are made automatically.

2. App Features

The Utility Tool Box app includes the following tools operating locally on your device:

  • ZIP File Creator & Extractor
  • Unit Converter & Age Calculator
  • Compass & Mobile Info Viewer
  • QR Code Generator, Scanner & UPI QR Generator
  • Notes, Notepad, Stopwatch & Calculator
  • Image Compressor and Image Tools
  • Emergency Dial Feature & Wi-Fi Direct Nearby Sharing
  • Password Generator

Note: Each tool operates locally on your device and does not send any data to external servers.

3. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for users aged 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

4. Data Security

We value your trust. Your data stays completely on your device. No data is shared with any external database, and the app is 100% safe and secure.

5. Advertising

The app may display ads from third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies to personalize ad content, but Utility Tool Box itself does not store or use this data.
